Research Poster

We were tasked with picking a subject to reasearch and create a poster using code.

It was obligated to have certain information sections and certain styles of portraying such with visuals or references from studies.

Python

IDEAS

We had to get into groups and create a Python Project. We had to come up with an idea for an app that would solve an issue. Me and my partner chose the idea of a travel destination finder.

Research

Lots of research went into the code since me and my friend were not very advanced in python and we wanted to use the built in turtle feature instead of tkinter. Our teacher helped with a lot of our problem solving but we eventually pulled through. 

OUTCOME

The final outcome is a desktop pop-up  that shows a selection of categories to choose from. Each category contains 2 travel destination ideas with some popular tourist attractions.
